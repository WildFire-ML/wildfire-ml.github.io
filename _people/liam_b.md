Liam is a Research Associate in Spatial Modeling (Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering) and Grant Manager at the University of Alberta. His work focuses on developing practical applications of machine learning and data science methods for remote sensing data (drone, aerial, and satellite) to solve geospatial problems and enhance decision-making, with an emphasis on forestry and wildfire science. [His personal website can be found here.](https://liambennett.net/academic/)
